Banana Bread in a Mug

This banana bread in a mug is a fast-to-make, moist, and fluffy dessert. It's also a great way to use up overripe bananas, which are sweeter than unripe bananas - so they work perfectly in this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Â½ ripe banana
  • 1 Tbsp (15 mL) unsweetened soy milk
  • 1 Tbsp (15 mL) vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp (5 mL) pure vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3 Tbsp (26 g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tbsp (7 g) ground flaxseeds
  • 1 Tbsp (14 g) granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbsp (9 g) chopped dairy-free dark chocolate

Directions

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mash the banana with a fork. Then add the milk, oil, vanilla, and salt and mix.
  2. Add the flour, flaxseeds, sugar, chocolate, and baking powder and mix until just incorporated. Be careful not to over-mix.
  3. Microwave at high power (800 - 900 W) for 2 minutes* or until a toothpick inserted into the centre comes out clean. If the cake isn't cooked through yet, cook at intervals of 15 seconds until done. Add your desired toppings and enjoy!
